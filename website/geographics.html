Geo Graphics is a compiled work created by Monash University design students. This book will combine the projects of students in the digital publishing unit. The works therein will demonstrate several interpretations of 'The Stack'.
The Stack is the idea that our everyday lives are inseparable from technology and the onslaught of information.
This chapter involves the idea of the online casino. The gambling world has been revolutionised by technology. It is brighter, louder, and more addictive than ever. This chapter aims to communicate the sensory overload and candy coated glitz of the industry.
